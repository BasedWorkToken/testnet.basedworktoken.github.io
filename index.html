A way to securely mint Base Work Tokens using our safe POW system and contracts.
Machines connected to the contract solve complicated hashes with an answer unique to their ethereum address for the reward.
Miners recieve 25 Base Work Tokens per mint able to bundle the mints up to 2016 times in one transaction!

MultiMinting custom configurations change MaxBWORKperMint and MinBWORKperMint to decide how frequently to mint your bundle of mints in 1 transaction.

To do so just change "MinBWORKperMint": 250, in the BaseWorkToken.conf text file.

Change this setting in your BaseWorkToken.conf file by changing the "MinBWORKperMint" setting.
To Solo mine you must fund your account with Ethereum and supply the privateKey to BaseWorkToken.conf in order to mint with a GPU, we suggest a fresh account with just mining funds
